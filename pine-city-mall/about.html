About The Mall

Welcome to Pine City Mall, a luxury shopping destination nestled in the heart of Centurion, South Africa. Here, we pride ourselves on offering an unparalleled shopping experience, featuring top clothing brands and boutique stores that cater to the discerning tastes of our clientele. Step into our world-renowned cinema theater, where you can indulge in the latest blockbuster hits in the comfort of reclining chairs, complete with a bar and onsite kitchen for your enjoyment. Our commitment to providing world-class entertainment extends beyond the silver screen, ensuring that every visit is nothing short of extraordinary.

Savor the flavors of excellence at our array of restaurants, offering diverse dining experiences that showcase the finest culinary creations. Whether you're craving international cuisine or local delicacies, our establishments promise to delight your palate and elevate your dining experience. At Pine City Mall, we prioritize the safety and comfort of our guests. With ample parking facilities, kids' play areas, and childcare facilities, we ensure that every member of the family is catered to.

Plus, dont miss the opportunity to explore our newly established zoo, located nearby, for a memorable and enriching experience.
Join us at Pine City Mall, where luxury meets leisure, and every moment is an opportunity to indulge in the extraordinary.

Security

Fidelity Security Services provides round-the-clock manned security at Pine City Shopping Mall. Our highly trained guards offer 24/7 onsite assistance, ensuring the safety and well-being of all visitors.

With security patrols operating 24/7, 365 days a year, you can shop with peace of mind knowing that your safety is our top priority. Our guards are equipped with radio communication linked to a central control center, allowing for continuous monitoring of the CCTV systems that cover the shopping Mall.
